How to deploy bot on HEROKU

Make sure you edit all of these yourself in your app.json or Heroku Settings:

REJECT_CALL
AUTO_BLOCK
STATUS_READ_MSG
AUTO_STATUS_REPLY
AUTO_STATUS_SEEN
ANTI_DELETE
AUTO_REACT
ALWAYS_ONLINE
AUTO_RECORDING
AUTO_TYPING
AUTO_READ
OWNER_NUMBER
PREFIX
SESSION_ID
Important: On all of these, use true for ON and false for OFF.
